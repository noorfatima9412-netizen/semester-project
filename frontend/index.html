Pharmacy Management System

A simple web-based system to manage medicines, customers, sales, prescriptions, and pharmacy operations in one place.

Get Started Learn More

System Features

📦 Medicine Inventory

Add, view, update, and delete medicines. Track stock quantity and expiry dates.

👥 Customer Management

Register customers and keep their contact details and purchase history.

💰 Sales & Billing

Create sales records with automatic price calculation and stock update.

📋 Prescriptions

Record prescriptions with dosage, duration, and doctor information.

📊 Dashboard

View total medicines, sales, customers, and low stock alerts at a glance.

🔍 Search & Filter

Search medicines by name or category. Find expiring medicines quickly.
